## Concept Overview
The Chain of Thought Reasoning icon represents the sequential multi-step inference process used by large language models to arrive at a conclusion. Rather than jumping directly from input to output, the design abstracts the intermediate reasoning steps that build context and logical depth at each stage.

## Visual Interpretation

A abstract geometric diagram illustrating the Chain of Thought reasoning process used in large language models. The design features a vertical sequential flow of processing blocks, each containing a central node flanked by structured text lines, connected by progressively growing circular nodes that represent accumulating context and reasoning depth. The chain begins with a plain input block at the top, passes through multiple reasoning steps, and terminates in a large output circle, symbolizing the convergence of multi-step inference into a final conclusion.
